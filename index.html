    Instructions

    To see the how WebSockets work in more detail, follow these steps in Chrome.

    1. Type your message in the text field.
    2. Press 'Send Message'
    3. Open dev tools (F12)
    4. Navigate to the 'Network' tab.
    5. Click on the 'echo.websockets.org file.
    6. Click on the 'Messages' tab to see the echo.
